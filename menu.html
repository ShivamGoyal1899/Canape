About Canapé

Canapé deals with the technological aspect in solving the problem of food wastage in college mess at individual level. The proposed concept can be implemented by universities and colleges in their mess to provoke students to save food and therefore save money. The solution basically involves an Android Application which handles the whole Mess Management in a concise manner.
